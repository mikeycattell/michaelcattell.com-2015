Here’s what I can do for you.

Since graduating in 2003, I've been designing and coding websites, in that time I’ve worked for all types of companies, ranging from advertising agencies to selling bow ties for dogs!

I pride myself on being a 50/50, a designer and a front-end developer - so this means not only can I create something visually pleasing and perfect for your customers, but I can also take the signed off designs and code them for launch.

What type of work do you take on?

This is an easy question to answer, anything! If the project is interesting to you, then it is interesting to me.
I can just do the design, or just the front-end development - or in most cases I can do the complete package. All my work is responsive, so it can be easily accessed on any device.
I code the website in clean and readable html/css and jQuery and do my mock ups in Photoshop.

What’s It like to work together?

One word: Fun.
I strive to make the project as enjoyable for both sides, so from a friendly initial meeting (of course with coffee), to the final launch - I will support, advise, and take leadership (if required) and get you your new shiny website live.

What's your process like?

This is where I earn my points: after the initial meeting, I then go straight into research mode; this involves discovery, gaining inspiration - spying on your competitors to find out what they do well or wrong and then its time to knuckle down and get my design on.
You will receive an estimated project delivery date, and I keep in touch during my whole time working - this is usually to keep you updated on progression, or if I need any answers “on-the-go” or if you have questions you need answering.

Honesty is my way

Honestly is my middle name - well, this isn't true (it's Alan if you cared), but I pride myself on being honest and I try to bring out your honesty - if you don't like something, it's always welcome to have an opinion about it. I am upfront about the complete process, from initial payment to end deliverables. Anytime you are not happy or need answers/reassurance I am available.


Now that is all over - should we have a quick chat about that wonderful idea you have and want to get it online?
