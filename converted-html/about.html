Nice to meet you

I’m Michael. You can call me Michael, Mike or Mikey. I live in a small town called Doncaster in South Yorkshire. I have worked all around England, with a prolonged stopover in Orange County, California - which part of me still considers home.

I’ve worked with small to large agencies, “mama and papa” companies to full blown companies with their own coffee machines and Spotify playlists.

I like to think I specialise in interface and experience design, combining digital craftsmanship with innovative thinking to deliver user-first digital solutions.

I am very passionate with all the work I do, some could say borderline obsessive (Is there really a difference?).
I’ve been doing this for about 10 years now, either riding solo, part of teams, inside wonderful agencies based all over England with lovely coffee. What this means is, I’ve seen a lot, created a lot, sworn a lot and typed a lot of code, but wouldn’t change a thing.

I don’t pretend to do everything, and I'm very honest about this, but what I can promise is a product of the highest standard which you can use and be proud of. I specialise in design, development (front-end development), guidance and support, which when all added up give you the complete experience to launch a great product.
