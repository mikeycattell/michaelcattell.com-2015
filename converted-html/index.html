I design and develop fresh, unique and lively designs for web and mobile

My name is Michael Cattell, and I’m a digital designer and front-end developer with a love for the web, interaction design, and build. I like designing and building for all types of businesses, whilst drinking really good coffee.

I work from my sunny (or mostly rainy) home studio based in South Yorkshire, which I share with my wife, and a very fluffy Pomeranian called Ralph. I work for all small to large businesses, specialising in designing clean interfaces, as well as delivering a responsive web site to work on all devices.

SERVICES

— Design

Great companies always have memorable and consistent identities to connect with their audiences. I find out what makes your company' users tick, then I create clean, usable and great user experiences.

— Web Build

For me, an online presence is key to a company's success. I create a brand online using the latest technological standards, including responsive design and a mobile-first philosophy. Ensuring your company will thrive online and be a success.

— Full Support

Our friendship won’t end either. I provide you with constant support throughout the entire process, including when your site has gone live (or you just can’t figure out what to have for dinner tonight).
